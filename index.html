Our Aim

According to recent studies in Kerala, our future generation is prone to lifestyle diseases due to the lack of fitness and poor eating habits. Inorder to save our future generation from this, we need to bring changes in their lifestyle. Inorder to bring this change, Youngstiny is bringing to you an event called GAMETHETICS. GAMETHETICS KID'S in Gamethetics, we are focusing on health and fitness of young kids of age 3-6 with a fun event which is a combination of athletics and games. By preparing them for this event, they can enhance their fitness by having fun with the spirit of competition!
